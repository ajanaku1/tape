# Tape - terminal visual language

**Color mode:** dark-only (finance/trust terminal - dark is the identity).
**Brand words:** verifiable · immutable · forensic · precise · auditable.
**Tagline:** *The tape doesn't lie.*

**Mood:** a forensic trading terminal. Numbers feel measured, monospaced, exact.
The signature interaction is **drill-down to proof** - every metric expands to its
Walrus blobId + Sui txn + Clock timestamp, with a one-click **Verify** that re-derives
the number client-side. The signature moment is the **✓ Verified vs ✗ Claimed** contrast.

**Signature treatments**
- A "verify" affordance with a state machine: idle → verifying (re-fetching blob, re-hashing) → ✓ matched.
- Mono numerals (tabular figures); generous use of a single accent for "verified" green.
- Proof chips: `blob:1JKd…tNs` and `tx:2BEu…Y2FZ` are clickable, copyable, monospaced.

**Three directions (gate)**
- **A - Bloomberg-dense terminal:** maximum information density, dark slate, amber/green accents, panels + ticker. For power users / judges who want everything on one screen.
- **B - Institutional audit report:** calm, document-like, lots of whitespace-on-dark, a serif display face for headings; reads like a signed attestation. For the "this is credible infrastructure" feel.
- **C - Consumer receipts:** bold oversized numbers, card/receipt metaphor, the verified badge is the hero. For instant legibility and shareability.

DNA tokens are defined inline per proposal file.
